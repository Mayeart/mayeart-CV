About Me

A story of growth

I am a person passionate about programming and website design, graduated from the Simón Bolívar University of Venezuela, specialist in Electronic Technology. My beginnings included electives like internet and digital communication protocols. I have 8 years of experience in electronic control systems applied to the oil industry, work that allowed me to develop project management, teamwork, effective communication, and strengthened my passion for design and innovation in programming. Later I specialized in design and web development training at ITLA in front-end areas with Angular and Python, and since then I have worked as a freelancer in these areas while continuing my training.
